About Confluence

Confluence – our annual international conference on teaching and learning English as a Second Language (ESL) – serves as a platform for dialogue and debate with researchers and theorists on one hand and ESL teachers and learners, on the other. There is a pressing need to bring both sides of the expertise continuum together; Confluence aims to address this need and explore the myriad aspects of ESL.

The conference offers an opportunity for researchers, academics and classroom practitioners from across the world to share their expertise and advice.

Confluence was first presented in 2010.
